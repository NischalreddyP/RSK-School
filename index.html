About Us

Back in 1995, when there were hardly any educational institutions in and around Chintal; when there were difficulties to commute to schools that provide quality education; when education was a privilege to few people in the locality; our visionary founders K. Rameswar Reddy and K. Aruna Reddy, identified the gap in the locality, established R.S.K. High School to serve children around the institution with finest quality education with innovative and child centric learning. We are proud to announce that R.S.K. High School was first school in Chintal to setup Computer lab, Science lab and Mathematics lab inside the campus. We are also the first in locality to introduce digital classrooms to all the classes.

With over 25 years of experience in creating stars that create an impact in the society, we will continue creating stars that will be part of India’s progress.
